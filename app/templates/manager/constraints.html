How constraints work
Hard constraints are pass/fail filters. Any employee who fails even one constraint is eliminated from consideration for this role. The engine then scores only the remaining candidates.

Use Mandatory Skill to require a skill at any level.
Use Min Proficiency to require a skill at a specific level (1-10).
Use Max Workload to exclude overloaded employees.
